Savannah River IPA

A spunky India Pale Ale. It uses hops like Simcoe, Cascade and a few others to make a fun hoppy flavor. All home town breweries need great tasting fun IPA.

Dynamite Brown Ale

A hoppy American Brown Ale. It has a mild malty taste accentuated with a mixture of American hops that is like “sugar and spice” so good, so good.

Green Jacket Pilsner

Brewed in the tradition of Czech Pilsners, light and crisp with just the right amount of Saaz hops, making it delicately balanced and an easy “pil” to swallow, the mark of a true champion.

Westobou Amber Ale

A prideful red ale, named in the tradition of the Westobou River, now known as the Savannah River. It has an extra helping of Chinook hops that balances out the complex malt flavors.
